Interface Redesign for Eversense

Brief: Redesigning the User Interface of their Machine Monitoring Product: Eversense

2020, Bdes. Graduation Project, Commercial
Sponsor: Forbes Marshall Pvt. Ltd, Pune

Design Solution: A new interface for the application & a Design System (Guidelines) for future development of the product.

Design Process:
The existing interface was studied through many different points of view. After understanding the user’s needs, a vision for the redesign was formulated. A new structure was decided for the application.

The wireframe for the redesigned interface was obtained through a highly iterative process of sketching & testing options.

A new product identity was devised to better reflect the product vision. Based on the new look & feel, Design Guidelines were created in the form of an interactive website which will be used by future designers and developers of the product.
